## Goal
Produce an evidence-backed recommendation for how PALS should deepen onboarding and product-definition capture in Phase 67 without prematurely locking in the wrong durable artifact model or ballooning hot-path context costs.

Phase 66 improved collaboration behavior, but Phase 67 needs to decide something more foundational before implementation: what durable product/project information PALS should preserve, how that information should be read across the lifecycle, and whether PROJECT.md and PRD-depth material should live in one artifact, two artifacts, or a layered model.
