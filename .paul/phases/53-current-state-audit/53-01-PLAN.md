<objective>
## Goal
Produce a complete, evidence-backed audit of the current PALS Pi port, covering skills, slash commands, extension API usage, installer/uninstaller behavior, artifact compatibility strategy, and overall fit with Pi’s documented capabilities.

## Purpose
Phase 53 establishes the factual baseline for the milestone. Before recommending changes, PALS needs a clear picture of what the Pi port currently does, what Pi actually supports, where the port is already strong, and where the implementation may be thin, incorrect, or underusing native Pi features.

## Output
- `.paul/phases/53-current-state-audit/pi-port-inventory.md` — implementation inventory of all Pi-specific PALS assets and their current behavior
- `.paul/phases/53-current-state-audit/pi-capability-fit-analysis.md` — comparison of current implementation against Pi docs/examples for skills, commands, extensions, hooks, and packaging/installation model
- `.paul/phases/53-current-state-audit/53-current-state-audit-report.md` — synthesized current-state audit with strengths, risks, open questions, and transition notes for Phase 54
</objective>
