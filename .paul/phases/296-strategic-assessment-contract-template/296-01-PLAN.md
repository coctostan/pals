<objective>
## Goal
Author the contract-first foundation for v2.70: a `docs/PALS-STRATEGIC-ASSESSMENT-CONTRACT.md` that defines the optional strategic-assessment checkpoint (five evidence lenses, capability-detection rules, output format, and non-authoritative authority stance), plus the `kernel/templates/STRATEGIC-ASSESSMENT.md` output-artifact template the future workflow will instantiate under `.paul/assessments/`.

## Purpose
PALS jumps straight from milestone completion to "what do you want to build next?" with no standing step that takes stock of project reality first. This phase establishes the authoritative implementation boundary (the contract) and the durable output shape (the template) so Phase 297 can wire the checkpoint into `discuss-milestone.md` against a fixed spec rather than inventing structure inline. Contract-first matches the project's recent cadence (v2.47/v2.48/v2.49/v2.50/v2.65) and systematizes the ad-hoc "take stock + rank next target" move proven manually in Phase 295.

## Output

- `kernel/templates/STRATEGIC-ASSESSMENT.md` — new output-artifact template for `.paul/assessments/{date}-{slug}.md`.
- No workflow wiring, no validation harness changes (those are Phases 297/298).
