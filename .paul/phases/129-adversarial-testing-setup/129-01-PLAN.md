## Goal

v2.24 proved modules dispatch in a single-model, single-session test. This phase proves PALS works under real multi-model pressure with adversarial payloads designed to trigger many modules simultaneously.

## Output
- `adversarial-results.md` — structured comparison of both loops across all three assessment levels
- Raw `.paul/` artifacts from each loop preserved in pi-verify for evidence
</objective>
