<objective>
## Goal
Wire optional, non-blocking generation/surfacing of **milestone** and **PLAN** HTML presentation packets into the milestone-creation and plan-phase workflows, instantiating `kernel/templates/HTML-PRESENTATION-PACKET.md` against the fixed `docs/PALS-HTML-PRESENTATION-PACKETS-CONTRACT.md` boundary.

## Purpose
Phase 299 fixed the packet contract, storage convention, packet types, audience modes, and static HTML template. Phase 300 makes the first two packet types real: a reviewer can opt into a milestone-context brief at milestone creation and a PLAN-review brief at plan review. Generation mirrors the v2.70 strategic-assessment precedent — optional, default-skip, main-session, source-cited, durable-but-non-hot-path — so reviewability improves without reviving live UI, weakening `.paul/*` authority, or making packets decide lifecycle state. APPLY/UNIFY packets (Phase 301) and validation guardrails (Phase 302) stay out of scope.

## Output
- `kernel/workflows/create-milestone.md` — new optional `render_milestone_packet` step that instantiates the template as a `milestone` packet under `.paul/presentation-packets/{milestone-slug}/milestone-brief.html`.
- `kernel/workflows/plan-phase.md` — `review_plan` step extended with an optional PLAN-packet render that writes `.paul/presentation-packets/{NN}-{phase}/{NN}-{plan}-plan.html`.
- No APPLY/UNIFY wiring, no committed `.html` instances, no `drivers/*` or `tests/*` changes.
</objective>
