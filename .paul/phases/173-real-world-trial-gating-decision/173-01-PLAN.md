## Goal
Run CODI v0.1 against real PALS planning evidence already in the repo PLUS one representative TS-touching counterfactual scope, score it against the four success signals from the v2.39 milestone thesis with a CODI-null-aware rubric, and produce a binding ship-v0.2 / iterate-v0.1 / kill decision artifact. This phase closes v2.39 and gates *all* CODI follow-on work (`symbol_graph` v0.2, `trace` v0.3, PLAN.md template formalization v0.4, lifecycle expansion v0.5+).
## Purpose
The v2.39 milestone explicitly held back v0.2+ scope until v0.1 is judged on evidence. Phases 170â€“172 shipped the smallest possible vertical: registry-driven scaffolding (170), live `impact` integration with partial-success invariant (171), default-on distribution + prose-heavy seed coupling (172). Phase 173 is the honesty checkpoint: did v0.1 demonstrably improve PLAN quality, or are we paying for plumbing that does not change planner behavior?

A pre-plan empirical probe during this PLAN's construction surfaced a structural finding that reshapes the trial: CODI returned `0 resolved, 4 unresolved` on this plan's markdown-layer symbols (`impact`, `analyze_scope`, `pals-implementer`, `modules.codi`), but a follow-up probe on TS symbols from `drivers/pi/extensions/pals-hooks.ts` (`palsHooks`, `renderCompactLoopSummary`, `renderLifecycleStatus`, `syncLifecycleUi`) returned 3 of 3 with real downstream call-sites at depth 1â€“3 and fan-out up to 21. The codegraph DOES index PALS TypeScript; the trial sample's 0/4 resolution reflects extractor-vs-scope alignment, not a CODI failure or a non-representative-repo problem. The trial therefore needs at least one TS-touching counterfactual session to test whether CODI's value envelope holds where the codegraph index actually applies. Phase 168 (core lifecycle UI polish) touched exactly that file pre-CODI and is the natural counterfactual.

This is the same gating discipline the source integration plan called out â€” no v0.2 work begins until the v0.1 trial answers it, and the trial is now structured so the answer is not predetermined by sample selection.

1. `173-01-TRIAL-DATA.md` â€” raw trial dataset spanning 4 retrospective sessions (170-01, 171-01, 172-01, 173-01-self-trial) PLUS one TS-touching counterfactual session (Phase 168 scope re-run as a CODI dry-run against the live codegraph index).
