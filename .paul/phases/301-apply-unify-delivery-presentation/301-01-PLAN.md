<objective>
## Goal
Wire optional, non-blocking generation of APPLY and UNIFY HTML presentation packets into `kernel/workflows/apply-phase.md` and `kernel/workflows/unify-phase.md`, instantiating the Phase 299 contract/template at the finalized APPLY and UNIFY lifecycle moments without touching lifecycle authority, mirroring the milestone/PLAN packet wiring shipped in Phase 300.

## Purpose
Completes the v2.71 packet-type coverage (milestone, PLAN, APPLY, UNIFY) so reviewers can read a static HTML brief at the two delivery moments — "what changed and what passed" (APPLY) and "does the SUMMARY reconcile and is the phase ready to close" (UNIFY) — while `.paul/*` and PLAN/APPLY/UNIFY remain the only authoritative lifecycle truth. This advances the milestone core value: derived briefs, authoritative artifacts.

## Output
- `kernel/workflows/apply-phase.md`: an optional, non-blocking APPLY-packet offer at the finalized APPLY moment (after STATE PLAN ✓ → APPLY ✓ → UNIFY ○, before the Continue-to-UNIFY routing prompt), plus contract/template references and required-reading entries.
- `kernel/workflows/unify-phase.md`: an optional, non-blocking `render_unify_packet` step after `finalize_summary` and before `github_flow_merge_gate`, plus contract/template references and required-reading entries.
- No committed `.html` packet instances; no validation-harness, driver, or doc-contract changes.
</objective>
