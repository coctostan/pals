<objective>
## Goal
Turn the completed Phase 158 artifact audit and Phase 159 workflow audit into one decision-usable findings package that ranks context-reduction options for PALS under Pi usage.

## Purpose
Phase 160 closes the v2.35 milestone by moving from measurement to recommendation. The project already knows that artifact-side cost and workflow-side amplification are separate but compounding pressures. This phase matters because the next milestone needs a bounded, evidence-backed answer about what to trim first, what to preserve, and which structural changes are worth future consideration without weakening artifact-first lifecycle authority.

## Output
- `.paul/phases/160-findings-recommendation-package/context-bloat-findings-matrix.md` — combined evidence matrix covering artifact-side and workflow-side findings, confidence level, hot/warm/cold relevance, and fix leverage
- `.paul/phases/160-findings-recommendation-package/context-reduction-recommendations-ranked.md` — ranked tactical-now and structural-later reduction recommendations with rationale, expected value, risk, and authority-safety posture
- `.paul/phases/160-findings-recommendation-package/160-findings-recommendation-package-report.md` — final milestone-close report summarizing the strongest recommendations, what should not change, and the recommended next-step sequence
</objective>
