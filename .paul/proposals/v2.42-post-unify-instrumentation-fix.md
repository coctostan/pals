# v2.42 CODI v0.1 — Post-Unify Instrumentation Fix

**One-line premise:** v2.41 answered the natural-scope value question qualitatively but exposed that CODI's post-unify measurement surface is not trustworthy enough to support clean mechanical gating. v2.42 is a bounded milestone to repair that measurement surface — **not** to reopen CODI's advisory behavior and **not** to reinterpret v2.41's trial record.

---

## Why this milestone, why now

Phase 179 closed with three durable findings:
1. CODI pre-plan advisory materially shaped APPLY on natural scope.
2. quark `112-01` exposed a **parser-miss** failure mode in the post-unify instrumentation.
3. quark `113-01` exposed a distinct **hook-not-firing** failure mode in the same measurement surface.

Phase 180 then applied the unchanged Phase 173/177 rubric and closed v2.41 with verdict **ITERATE_V0_1**. The verdict did **not** route to KILL — the natural evidence is too strong for that — but it also did **not** route to SHIP because the mechanical ledger remains instrument-limited. The next milestone therefore should repair the measurement surface before any v0.2 scope expansion.

---

## Motivation

### Finding 1 — Parser-miss on quark `112-01`

The quark Phase 112 PLAN carried a real CODI Blast Radius and the quark Phase 112 SUMMARY explicitly states **CODI materially shaped APPLY: yes**. But the post-unify hook wrote:

- `| 112-01 | 2026-04-18 | no-dispatch-found | — | — | — | — | n |`

The recorded reason is that the plan contained the injected Blast Radius section, but **not the exact success-log string** the instrumentation parser expected. This is a measurement failure, not absence of value.

Primary sources:
- pals `.paul/phases/179-organic-observation/179-01-SUMMARY.md`
- quark `.paul/phases/112-security-correctness-runtime-wiring/112-01-SUMMARY.md`
- quark `.paul/CODI-HISTORY.md`

### Finding 2 — Hook-not-firing on quark `113-01`

The quark Phase 113 PLAN records a clean pre-plan CODI injection:

- `impact × 5 symbols → 2 resolved-with-call-sites, 3 resolved-but-empty, 0 unresolved, 5 total call-sites, injected blast_radius`

But the quark Phase 113 SUMMARY's `## Module Execution Reports` section contains WALT / SKIP / RUBY only — **no CODI post-unify entry at all** — and quark `.paul/CODI-HISTORY.md` never received a `113-01` row. Again, this is a measurement failure, not absence of value.

Primary sources:
- pals `.paul/phases/179-organic-observation/179-01-SUMMARY.md`
- quark `.paul/phases/113-security-correctness-ii/113-01-PLAN.md`
- quark `.paul/phases/113-security-correctness-ii/113-01-SUMMARY.md`
- quark `.paul/CODI-HISTORY.md`

### Combined effect

Two consecutive natural phases produced **two successful pre-plan dispatches** and only **one mechanical ledger row**, with the surviving row itself misclassified as `no-dispatch-found`. That is a 2× undercount from two different failure modes. v2.42 exists to remove that censoring effect from future measurement.

## Scope

v2.42 is **strictly bounded** to the post-unify instrumentation / measurement surface.

### In scope

1. **Parser hardening**
   - Make the post-unify classifier robust to the qualitative wording patterns already used in real PLAN/SUMMARY artifacts.
   - Re-check plan-path resolution and fallback behavior (`PLAN.md <module_dispatch>` primary, SUMMARY fallback, hotfix fallback).
   - Tighten outcome classification so legitimate injected outcomes are not collapsed into `no-dispatch-found` simply because wording varies slightly.

2. **Hook-firing guard**
   - Investigate why CODI post-unify dispatched for `112-01` but not for `113-01`.
   - Check hook registration, unify workflow dispatch sequencing, installed-registry alignment, and any assumptions about when SUMMARY regions already exist.
   - Add a bounded guard so a silent non-fire becomes detectable rather than invisible.

3. **Installer / manifest alignment if root cause lives there**
   - If the hook-not-firing path turns out to be installed-registry or manifest-shape drift, repair only that bounded alignment surface.
   - Keep the fix at the measurement layer; do not widen into general module architecture redesign.

### Explicitly out of scope

- CODI pre-plan advisory behavior
- `impact` / codegraph value-envelope redesign
- `symbol_graph`, `trace`, or any v0.2 expansion
- broader module-system architecture changes
- retroactive edits to any v2.41 artifact or any `CODI-HISTORY.md` row

**Pinned at milestone open (2026-04-20):** `26f262c3af3b87221ba0872b55e89fdea7c02d52`

### Rules

1. Do **not** reuse v2.41's SHA as the v2.42 freeze.
2. Pin the new SHA exactly once at v2.42 milestone open.
3. Any instrumentation edits after that pin invalidate the current v2.42 trial slice and require an explicit integrity note.
4. v2.41 artifacts continue to be interpreted only against the v2.41 SHA, never against the repaired v2.42 instrument.

Rationale: v2.41's question was "what happened under the frozen instrument we actually ran?" v2.42's question is "does the repaired measurement surface now report honestly and consistently?" Those are different trial questions and must not share a freeze boundary.

## Rough milestone shape

### Phase A — Root-cause analysis

Goal: isolate the exact causes of:
- parser-miss on `112-01`
- hook-not-firing on `113-01`

Deliverables:
- a narrow defect map from real artifacts to code/manifest/workflow paths
- proof of which layer owns each failure
- decision on whether installer/registry alignment is in-scope

### Phase B — Bounded fix under fresh SHA freeze

Goal: implement the minimum repair required for honest post-unify measurement.

Deliverables:
- parser hardening and/or dispatch guard
- updated validation around the measurement surface
- fresh v2.42 SHA pin recorded at milestone open

### Phase C — Natural validation

Goal: run the repaired instrument on a new natural quark phase and confirm:
- pre-plan dispatch still works
- post-unify fires reliably
- classifier writes the correct outcome row
- no synthetic evidence or retroactive repair is required
